05 · Positioning

Why this works in Rayfield.

Rayfield sits on a busy arterial road with a spending, image-conscious customer base and few premium quick-service options. Gemi avoids the tired fast-food clichés of loud reds and plastic seating. Instead it offers a clean, photogenic space people want to be seen in and post about — turning every customer into free marketing.

Open-front bay Drive-by visibility Delivery-first Instagrammable
The one thing to remember

Strip everything back to black and white — then let bright Nigerian food be the only colour that shouts.

That single discipline is what makes the brand feel premium, scalable and unmistakably its own.
